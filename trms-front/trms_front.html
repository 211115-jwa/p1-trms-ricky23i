Tuition Reimbursement

Tuition Reimbursement Request

The purpose of Tuition Reimbursement is to provide a system that encourages quality knowledge growth relevant to an individual’s expertise. Each employee is allowed to claim up to $1000 in tuition reimbursement a year. The amount available to an employee is reset on the new year. Event types have different standard reimbursement coverage: University Courses 80%, Seminars 60%, Certification Preparation Classes 75%, Certification 100%, Technical Training 90%, Other 30%.
